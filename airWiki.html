Since 1984 (2023)

Air Jordan (also known as AJ's) is a renowned brand of basketball shoes, apparel, and accessories originally developed by Nike in collaboration with NBA legend Michael Jordan. Launched in 1984, Air Jordans quickly became a cultural phenomenon, transcending the realm of sports to become a symbol of style and urban fashion. These sneakers are highly regarded for their innovative designs, incorporating Nike's Air cushioning technology, which provides superior comfort and support for athletes. The brand has since released numerous iterations, each distinct in design and performance features, while preserving the iconic Jumpman logo. Air Jordans have amassed a massive global fan base and remain sought-after collectibles, resonating with both basketball enthusiasts and fashion-conscious individuals alike.
